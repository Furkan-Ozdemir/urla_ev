About the Property

Our detached 7+2 triplex house offers an extraordinary location for those seeking a peaceful seaside retreat. Located just 150 meters from the sea, nestled within a 320 square meter garden, it boasts sea and island views from its terraces and lush verandas. With two kitchens and a total of five bathrooms, it can comfortably accommodate up to 18 people.

Floor Layouts

Ground Floor

Comfortable living room with A/C, fireplace, and 8-person dining table. Modern kitchen, spacious front and back verandas with outdoor dining and seating. Includes a double bedroom with a workspace and a bathroom.

First Floor

Features three bedrooms including a 4-person suite with an en-suite youth room and master bedroom. All rooms are equipped with double beds, A/C, TVs, balconies, and bathrooms.

Attic (Top Floor)

An independent flat featuring a living room, open American kitchen, 6-person dining table, and a private terrace. Includes two bedrooms (one double, one twin), A/C, TV, and an integrated water filtration system.
